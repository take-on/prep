Rake it up

Mission Objective

Rake it up aims to rake up new and talented grads. It provides a way for graduates to show off their skills and also familiarise themselves with oppertunities at investec.

Features

  • Stay up to date with new company oppertunities and events
  • Create a profile for graduates and company employees
  • See a roadmap of your potential paths at various companies
  • Earn brownie points by participating in company events and building your profile
  • View current in demand skills
  • 24/7 Chatbot support
